Photo Booth rentals are a fun and affordable addition to any special event. Reserving one only takes 3-simple steps!

  • First Steps

    Submit a request to see if the date of your event is available. It's best to check availability even if you're in the early stages of planning as reservations are regularly booked.

  • PICK YOUR RENTAL PACKAGE

    Choose between renting by the hour or one of our all inclusive packages depending on the needs of your event! Our all inclusive packages are ideal for longer events (4+ hours) that may have down time where guests aren't taking photos the entire time.
    (i.e.: Weddings, Graduation Parties etc...)

  • RESERVE YOUR BOOTH

    Once you've picked your package and decided the details of your event, we'll put everything in writing for you to reserve your booth with a deposit and signed contract.
