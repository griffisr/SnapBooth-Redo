  • What is the cost?

  • Can I customize the design of the photo strips to match my event theme??

  • Who takes the picture?

    The guests or the photo attendant. Since our booths are touchscreen, guests often opt for the 'Touch to start' option.

  • Can we personalize each photo with custom messages or overlays?

    Our photo booths offer customizable options, including adding custom messages, overlays, or logos to each photo. Make each snapshot unique and memorable by personalizing it to match your event theme or brand identity.

  • What backgrounds do you offer for your photo booth rentals?

    We offer a wide range of background options to suit any event theme or style. From classic solid colors to scenic landscapes and custom designs, you can choose the perfect backdrop to complement your event and enhance your photos.

  • Can we bring our own props to use with the photo booth??

    Absolutely! We encourage you to bring your own props to personalize the photo booth experience and add an extra touch of fun to your photos. Whether it's sentimental items, themed accessories, or branded props, feel free to bring along anything that reflects your unique style and personality.

  • How long is setup?

    Under 45 minutes under normal circumstances. In a pinch, we can get it up and be rolling in 20 minutes.

  • How do I access the digital photo gallery taken during my event?

    After your event, we provide you with a flash drive containing all the photos captured by the photo booth. Simply plug the flash drive into your computer to access and download your images, making it convenient to share and cherish your memories.

  • What is an open air photo booth?

    Just like a traditional photo booth only it is open on all sides and allows for guests to move about, jump, horseplay, and be wild. The sky is the limit!

  • Can we adjust the setup of the photo booth to accommodate larger groups if needed?
